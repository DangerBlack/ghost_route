Technology & Privacy

Ghost Route is a passthrough encrypted proxy. It does not log or store any information about your requests, nor is it responsible for the content of the provided URLs. All links are encrypted and only accessible to those with the generated link.

This design offers censorship resistance: the source URL is hidden from intermediaries, governments, or other entities. Only the encrypted link reveals the destination, making it difficult to block or monitor specific content. No browsing history or logs are kept, and the server does not retain any user data.
